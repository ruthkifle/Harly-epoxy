Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you take custom orders?

Yes, we do! You can customize colors, styles, and details to create a unique piece just for you.

Q: How long does it take to finish an order?

Most custom pieces take 3-5 business days to make, since every resin piece needs time to cure properly.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via bank transfer, Telebirr, and cash on delivery.

Q: Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery within some areas. Delivery fees may apply based on your location.

Q: Do you accept urgent orders?

We try our best to accommodate urgent requests. But resin needs time to cure, so urgent orders depend on the design. Message us to check availability.

Q: Do I need to make a prepayment to confirm my order?

Yes, we require a 50% prepayment to start working on your custom order. Since every item is custom-made, the prepayment helps us buy materials and secure your order. The remaining amount can be paid when your item is ready or upon delivery.
