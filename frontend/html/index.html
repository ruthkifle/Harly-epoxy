Why Choose Harly Resin

Every piece we make is designed to feel personal, meaningful, and made just for you.

  • Custom colors & styles – You can choose your favorite colors, flakes, and details to match your vibe or gift idea
  • Perfect for gifting – Our keychains, bookmarks, trays and plates make thoughtful, unique gifts for any occasion
  • Locally made in Addis Ababa – You’re supporting a small creative brand and helping us grow our dream.
  • Made to be used every day – Our designs are beautiful, but also practical, so you can enjoy them in your daily life.

How To Order

Getting your custom resin item is easy — just follow these steps.

  1. Choose the product category (Keychains, Bookmarks, Phone Stands, Trays, Plates, etc.)
  2. Pick your favorite design from our catalog
  3. Note the design code or send us a screenshot
  4. Tell us your preferred colors or custom details
  5. Send your order through Instagram, Telegram, TikTok, or Facebook
  6. We ask for a 50% advance payment to confirm the order
  7. Please place your order 3-5 days early so we can craft it with care
  8. Once your piece is ready, we’ll send final photos before delivery

Resin Care Steps to Keep Your Pieces Safe

  • Avoid extreme heat
  • Clean with soft cloth
  • Avoid scratching
